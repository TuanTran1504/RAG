The Student Community The Student Community
Western Sydney University International College engages four students each semester to represent the cohort. The Student Representative Council (SRC) are the student voice and are actively involved at students events and workshops on campus. The SRC work closely with International College staff to ensure that all students are represented.
At Western Sydney University International College, your success is our top priority. We are committed to providing you with an exceptional educational experience, and to ensure that you thrive, we offer a comprehensive range of student support services.
We welcome students from all corners of the globe, fostering a truly international environment. This diverse mix of students not only enhances the learning experience but also provides a global perspective that prepares you for an interconnected world.
In this multicultural setting, you’ll have the chance to engage in cross-cultural exchanges, celebrations, and events that showcase the richness of our collective experiences. Our commitment to diversity goes beyond the classroom, as we believe that learning extends to understanding and appreciating different perspectives.
If you are interested in joining the SRC, please refer to the policy here. You can also contact the Student Services team for further information. https://internationalcollege.westernsydney.edu.au/wp-content/uploads/student-representation-and-participation-policy.pdf
