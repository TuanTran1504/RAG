Under 18 Admissions Under 18 Admission
If you are under the age of 18 when planning to study at Western Sydney University International College, you must provide additional documentation about your accommodation and welfare.
Accommodation and welfare arrangements may be organised by parents or guardians, however the International College must approve these arrangements before final documentation can be issued. A local carer or guardian must be of good character and be over the age of 21. For more information on Homestay accommodation, click here.
Western Sydney University International College can provide you with the relevant forms and information if you intend to study as a minor. Please email mypathway@westernsydney.edu.au
