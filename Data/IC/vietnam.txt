The Diploma in Business is an extensive pathway program which provides a guaranteed entry into the second year of Western Sydney University Bachelor of Business or Bachelor of Accounting undergraduate degree. The Diploma in Business is offered in Vietnam by Western Sydney University International College under a third-party arrangement with the International School of Business (ISB) – University of Economics, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.
Students intending to study an International College course in Vietnam can find further information here. https://internationalcollege.westernsydney.edu.au/wp-content/uploads/Diploma-in-Business.pdf https://international.isb.edu.vn/dao-tao/diploma-in-business/
