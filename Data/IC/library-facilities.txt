Library Facilities Library Facilities
Western Sydney University International College students have access to state-of-the-art library facilities and a wide range of resources available for loan avaialble at all Western Sydney University campuses. The closest Western Sydney University campus library is at 169 Macquarie Street. In addition, the Parramatta Council also provides public library services.
Online resources are also available for all students 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. When visiting at campus in-person, you will need to bring your Western ID card to tap or swipe to access the library.
For more information on library services, please click here. https://library.westernsydney.edu.au/about_us/membership
