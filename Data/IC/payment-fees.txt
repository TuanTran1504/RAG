IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
In order to accept the offer, it is necessary to pay the fees and charges due, as indicated on the Letter of Offer.
Students have a number of options when it comes to paying your Western Sydney University International College tuition fees.
Payment of the amount due should be made in Australian dollars. When making payment students need to include their student ID, as listed in the Letter of Offer. Depending on the method of payment additional banking charges may apply.
It may take up to 5 business days for your payment to be received.
