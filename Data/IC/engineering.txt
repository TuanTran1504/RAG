Course Overview
The Diploma in Engineering is designed to engage, and further prepare you for, tertiary study in Engineering / Engineering Science, developing core skills in mathematical and physics knowledge and skills. The Diploma incorporates units equivalent to the first year of the Bachelor of Engineering or Bachelor of Engineering Science degrees.
Delivery mode: This course is delivered face-to-face/ on campus. Potential Careers
A Diploma in Engineering opens the door to a number of careers including:

Construction engineering
Structural design
Project management
Telecommunications
Energy generation and conservation
Road design, construction and management
