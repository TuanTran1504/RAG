Scholarships Scholarships For Pathway Students
Western Sydney University International College has introduced new scholarships for international pathway students from select countries. Scholarships are available for students who fulfil the scholarship requirements, and are subject to review. For more information, eligibility and the selection process, please contact mypathway@westernsydney.edu.au.
International College students are also eligible for scholarships from Western Sydney University upon transition to their undergraduate degree. These scholarships are automatically awarded to students based on their final pathway results.
