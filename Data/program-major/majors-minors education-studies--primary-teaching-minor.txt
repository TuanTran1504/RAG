- Education Studies – Primary Teaching, Minor (0296)
- Effective from:
- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- The Education Studies - Primary Teaching minor is comprised of the four initial subjects from the Master of Teaching (Primary). This minor is only available to students enrolled in 3756 Bachelor of Science (Pathway to Teaching Primary/Secondary) program. Students can only enrol in TEAC 7032 Diversity, Social Justice and Schooling when they have achieved 120 credit points in their overall program. Students can only enrol in the other three subjects in the minor; TEAC 7156 Aboriginal, Education: Past, Present and Future, TEAC 7092 Primary Human Society and its Environment and TEAC 7161 Educational Psychology for Learning and Teaching when they have achieved 160 credit points in their overall program.
