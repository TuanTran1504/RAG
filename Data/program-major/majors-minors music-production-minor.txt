- Music Production, Minor (0079)
- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- Previous Code: SM1126.1
- The Music Production minor allows Bachelor of Music students to develop expertise in studio recording and production, integrating music with multimedia, expanding live performance with technology and stage craft, and composing for screen media. The minor focuses on professional applications of music technology and builds students’ experience with creative projects undertaken solo or in groups.
