- This program applies to students who commenced in Term 1, 2021 or later.
- The Diploma in Health Science (Health and Physical Education) is available as an exit point only from 6001 - Diploma in Health Science/Bachelor of Health Science (Health and Physical Education).
- This program is designed to provide students with the first year subjects included in the Bachelor of Health Science (Health and Physical Education) program. It presents students with subjects covering introductory Science, Communication and Health aspects of the Bachelor of Health Science program and aims to produce students who are fully prepared for study beyond the first year of the B Health Science degree. It is delivered in an environment focused specifically on supporting students to make the transition into university study. Students who successfully complete the Diploma in Health Science (Health and Physical Education) will articulate into the B Health Science (Health and Physical Education) degree at WSU with up to one year (80 CPs) equivalent of advanced standing.
