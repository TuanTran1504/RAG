- Ubiquitous Computing, Minor (0325)
- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- Ubiquitous Computing is the concept of using small internet connections and inexpensive computers to help with everyday functions in an automated fashion. This minor introduces and explores the challenges Ubiquitous Computing presents in systems design and engineering, systems modelling, and user interface design.
