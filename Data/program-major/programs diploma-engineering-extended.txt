- This program is suspended from Term 1, 2025, no new students will be admitted from this session.
- Students should follow the program structure for the session start date relevant to the year they commenced.
- This program applies to students who commenced in Term 1, 2018 or later.
- This program is designed to provide students with the first year subjects included in the Bachelor of Engineering Science. The inclusion of additional preparatory subjects is designed to assist students in the transition to study at University level. The Diploma is delivered via extended face to face hours in smaller learning environments.
- Students who successfully complete this Diploma will articulate into Bachelor of Engineering Science with up to one year (80 CPs) equivalent of advanced standing.
- Study Mode
- One and a half years full-time (four terms).

- Admission
- This program is open to Australian Citizens and Permanent Residents who are aged 17 years or over and not currently enrolled in or completing Year 12 in the same year of intended admission.
- International Students
- IELTS 5.5 with minimum 5.0 in each sub band; or equivalent results from The College English Language Program or The College English Entrance Test; and completion of year 11 or equivalent with specified results.
