- The fourth industrial revolution, also known as Industry 4.0, is affecting almost every industry worldwide and rapidly transforming how businesses operate. The Undergraduate Certificate in Engineering is designed to provide basic employability skills and an introduction to the skills needed to navigate Industry 4.0. In addition to introducing students to the skills, knowledge, and attributes required for further studies in Engineering, students who successfully complete the Undergraduate Certificate will gain entry into the Diploma of Engineering.
