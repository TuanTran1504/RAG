- The Graduate Diploma in Global Security and Justice has a distinctive emphasis on human security and social justice imperatives. This program is for any students who are keen to develop and augment their critical thinking and ethical problem-solving skills, while gaining a robust understanding of international security and justice theories, policies, law, principles, and methods.
