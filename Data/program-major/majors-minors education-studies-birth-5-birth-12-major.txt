- Students enrolled in the 1708 Bachelor of Arts (Pathway to Teaching Birth-5/Birth-12) must complete an Education Studies Major to provide students with 80 credit points of expert content knowledge. Together with completion of subjects in the Master of Teaching (Birth-5 Years/Birth-12 Years) students are enabled to graduate from the Master of Teaching (Birth-5 Years/Birth-12 Years) with a qualification to teach in Early Childhood settings and Primary schools.
