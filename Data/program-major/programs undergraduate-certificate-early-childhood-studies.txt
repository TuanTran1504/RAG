- This program has been suspended from 2023.
- This program applies to students who commenced in Block C, 2021 or later.
- This program provides students with foundational knowledge in childhood developmental theory, psychology, and diversity, and mathematical principles required for educators. The program is designed as an entry qualification into the field of Early Childhood that will provide students with broad knowledge that they can apply to a professional context.
- This program provides a pathway for students into a Diploma of Arts Extended (Pathway to Teaching Birth-5 Birth-12).
