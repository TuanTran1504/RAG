- Space Science, Testamur Major (T030)
- Western Sydney University Major Code:
- Previous Code: ST3063.1
- Space Science is a fast-growing area in science with a broad range of applications. The Space Science major aims to equip graduates with advanced knowledge and skills in the disciplines of Space Science, Computing and Data Science. This major covers fundamental Space Science, statistical analysis, machine learning, deep learning, Data Science, ICT, Space Law, Astrophysics, as well as various Space Science applications. Students will also learn the basic theories and algorithms that are essential in the design and development of Space Science.
