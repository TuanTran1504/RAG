- Property, Minor (0289)
- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- Previous Code: SM2058.1
- In this minor on Property students learn the key concepts relating to property, property development, investment, and finance. These form the foundation for more in-depth topics on financial and risk assessment, and property portfolios. Through industry relevant activities students develop valuation reports and strategies for property investments and portfolios.
