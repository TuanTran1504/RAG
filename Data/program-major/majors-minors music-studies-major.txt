- Music Studies, Major (0343)
- Western Sydney University Major Code:
- The Music Studies major complements the core Bachelor of Music subjects to cover the four key areas of performance, composition, music production and musicology. The major includes subjects which explore cultural and intercultural contexts of music practice, while all subjects work alongside core creative and theoretical subjects towards practical applications in the capstone Intercultural Music Project.
