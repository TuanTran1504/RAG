- Screen Media Design, Minor (0204)
- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- Previous Code: SM1135.1
- This Screen Media Design minor comprises subjects that focus on principles of screen and sound media, video production and interactive design production as a tool for strategic communication. Students who complete this minor will satisfy the NESA requirements for teaching Graphics and Multimedia as a second teaching area within the Technological and Applied Studies discipline.
- Location
- Minor Structure
- Students must successfully complete 40 credit points as follows.
- Equivalent Subjects
- The subjects listed below count towards completion of this minor for students who passed these subjects in 2022 or earlier.
- COMM 2042 Visual Effects, replaced by COMM 2051 Animation and Visual Effects
- COMM 2051 Animation and Visual Effects
- COMM 2035 Screen and Sound Practices 1, replaced by COMM 1041 Screen Media Fundamentals
- COMM 3037 Transmedia Production, no longer offered as part of this minor
- COMM 1015 Fundamentals of Media Arts Production/COMM 1039 Introduction to Screen Media, no longer offered as part of this minor
