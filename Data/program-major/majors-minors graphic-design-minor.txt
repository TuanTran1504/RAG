- Graphic Design, Minor (0073)
- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- Previous Code: SM1118.1
- The Graphic Design minor provides students from outside the Design program with an introduction to fundamental skills and knowledge in graphic design. The minor includes a selection of practice-based and theoretical subjects and you can opt for study areas such as image design, contextual design studies, web design and branding. In addition, you will have the opportunity to choose a practice-based specialisation from the areas of interactive design; digital design; illustration and photomedia.
