- This program is designed to engage students in, and further prepare students for, tertiary study in science. It presents students with first year level Bachelor of Science subjects and aims to produce students who are fully prepared for study beyond the first year of the Bachelor of Science degree. It is delivered in a smaller, more supportive learning environment than usually found in first year undergraduate programs.
