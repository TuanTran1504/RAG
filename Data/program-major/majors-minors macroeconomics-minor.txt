- Macroeconomics, Minor (0293)
- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- Previous Code: SM2062
- Macroeconomics looks at the economy as a whole and the relations between different factors in the economy such as economic growth, inflation, and trade. Students learn about these economic factors or variables, the accounting framework, and about the value of macroeconomic models in managing economic change. Student have practice at interpreting and manipulating data, constructing macroeconomic models and transforming statistical data in a range of activities.
