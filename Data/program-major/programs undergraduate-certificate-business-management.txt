- To succeed in business, individuals must possess both the knowledge and know-how to create opportunities for professional advancement and the development of original and exciting initiatives. This program aims to harness students’ energy and passion for success by focussing on career and the future of enterprises in a range of contexts. This program provides an introduction to the necessary skills needed for engagement with the business world.
- Students who successfully complete this program will gain entry into the Diploma in Business with 40 credit points completed.
