- Business Regulation, Governance and Compliance, Major (0165)
- Western Sydney University Major Code:
- Previous Code: M2021.1
- A sound understanding of the legal framework of business regulation, governance and legal compliance is an increasingly important and expanding field of operation within a complex commercial world in which practitioners, to be effective and proactive, should acquire competencies of governance and risk assessment within a legal framework. The significant impact and interplay between law and business will be addressed with the objective of providing students with the skills to identify and critically evaluate the mandatory requirements for compliant business practice and the necessary strategies to minimise legal risk and maximise effective commercial governance. Students must be enrolled in Bachelor of Business or Diploma in Business/Bachelor of Business.
