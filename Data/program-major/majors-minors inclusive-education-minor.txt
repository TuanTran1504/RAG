- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- The Inclusive Education Minor provides students with a pathway to postgraduate studies to qualify as an inclusive education specialist teacher. Through a deep examination of current theory and practice, students develop the skills and knowledge necessary to work effectively with students, their families, and other professionals to support the inclusion of all students. The Inclusive Education Minor sits within the Transition to Practice phase of  Bachelor of Education programs.

- Minor Structure
- Students must have successfully completed 240 credit points in the program, including TEAC 3013 Inclusive Practices before enrolling in this minor.
