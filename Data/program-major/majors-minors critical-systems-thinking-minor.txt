- Critical and Systems Thinking, Minor (0287)
- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- Previous Code: SM8001
- Students in this Critical and Systems Thinking minor will embark on a journey to grow as creative thinkers who are globally aware, interculturally adept, and innovative changemakers. They will develop high-level critical, logical, and systems thinking skills and apply entrepreneurial mind-sets while examining challenges and opportunities from multiple perspectives, to propose innovative strategies that address complex contemporary and future challenges in both local and international contexts. These high-level thinking skills will allow the students to be adaptable and agile in the rapidly changing work-place.
- Students must have a minimum GPA of 5.0 to enrol in this minor.
- Students must complete 40 credit points as follows.
