Academic Handbook Current Edition REHA 3023 Occupational Therapy Clinical Practice 3b Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 400175 Coordinator Lee Zakrzewski Opens in new window Description This subject will allow students to consolidate academic knowledge and clinical skills. There will be opportunities to actively participate in assessment, analysis, goal setting, treatment/programme planning and occupational therapy intervention under the supervision of an occupational therapist. The placement will allow students to work for 5 consecutive weeks with occupational therapists in one of the many settings where therapists currently practice. School Health Sciences Discipline Occupational Therapy Student Contribution Band HECS Band 2 10cp Check your fees via the Fees page. Level Undergraduate Level 3 subject Pre-requisite(s) LGYA 7062 Incompatible Subjects LGYB 7771 - Clinical Placement 3 Learning Outcomes On successful completion of this subject, students should be able to: Demonstrate professional communication skills in the practice setting
Practice self-directed learning strategies through the use of a learning contract
Reflect on their practice experience using a structured format
Demonstrate professional behaviours and self management in a practice setting
Prepare documents in a professional format related to practice
Conduct and interpret assessments relevant to the practice area
Conduct occupational therapy intervention specific to the practice area Subject Content - professional behaviour
