Academic Handbook Current Edition LAWS 3067 Self-Managed Superannuation and Trusts Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 200900 Coordinator Leeanne Hodgson Opens in new window Description This subject provides students with the requisite skills to provide comprehensive financial advice, particularly within the context of an individual being able to choose a superannuation fund or create a self-managed superannuation fund (SMSF). The subject will provide students with an understanding of the implications of self-managed superannuation funds and the use of the trust structure, which are essential components for professionals in this area.
