- Advertising, Minor (0159)
- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- Previous Code: SM1123.1
- The Advertising minor provides a strong foundation in current advertising practice, exposing students to authentic agency experiences and sharpening their skills as communicators and persuaders. Areas of study include advertising strategy, media, copywriting and design, and campaigns. Students will develop the skills they need to succeed in a range of contexts, including campaign development, creative messaging and media planning.
