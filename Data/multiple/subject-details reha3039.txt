Academic Handbook Current Edition REHA 3039 Fluency and Voice Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 401308 Coordinator Leigha Dark Opens in new window Description This subject addresses the management of fluency disorders in children, adolescents and adults. It covers theoretical perspectives on stuttering and other fluency disorders, assessment and intervention principles, counselling and facilitating therapy, management of fluency disorders and indicators of successful change.
