- Economy and Society, Minor (0319)
- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- The Economy and Society minor is a multi-disciplinary social science academic program centred on human behaviour in the creation of sustainable economies. The minor will equip students with analytical skills to understand complex economic issues from a broader social science perspective. It will also examine the politics of policy making. This area of academic and intellectual interest complements any major from within the Bachelor of Social Science.
- Note: Subjects for this minor are offered on different campuses, potential students should ensure that they are able to manage their study and travel before enrolling in this minor.
