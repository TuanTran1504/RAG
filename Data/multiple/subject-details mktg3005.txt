Academic Handbook Current Edition MKTG 3005 Incubator 6: Funding and Start-up Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 301171 Coordinator Anton Bogdanovych Opens in new window Description This subject investigates various funding opportunities that might be suitable for your business concept through a number of theoretical topics, such as: possible funding sources including venture capitalists and angel investors, joint venture funding, pitching your ideas. The subject is structured into a number of modules. Further, as activities associated with this subject, students would have to actively seek and secure funding for the start-up.
