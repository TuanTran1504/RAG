Academic Handbook Current Edition BUSM 1010 Financing Enterprises Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 200910 Coordinator Gulay Avsar Opens in new window Description  Financing an enterprise plays an important role in ensuring its survival. Financing Enterprises focuses on the different types of enterprises available to start a business, financial statements issued by enterprises, key sources of finance available to small and large businesses, and how the surrounding financial and macroeconomic environments affect an enterprises performance. Participants in the subject will learn how to identify, analyse and interpret financial information using industry related database. The subject utilises problem solving and case studies so participants can understand the real world significance of finance. Successful completion of the subject equips participants with key concepts involved in financing enterprises.
