- Web Application Development (for Computing Students), Minor (0151)
- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- Previous Code: SM3056.1
- This minor provides a specialisation in developing systems specifically for the world wide web. The minor will enable students to develop systems for their own business or seek employment with a business that requires or already has a web presence. The minor is only available to students enrolled in the Bachelor of Computing or Bachelor of Information and Communications Technology programs.
