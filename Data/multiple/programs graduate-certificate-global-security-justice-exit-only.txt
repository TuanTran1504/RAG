- This program applies to students who commenced in Spring 2023 or later.
- Students should follow the program structure for the session start date relevant to the year they commenced.
- The Graduate Certificate in Global Security and Justice is interdisciplinary and encompasses domestic and international issues. It will equip students with critical thinking and ethical problem-solving skills that will remain resilient to rapid change across different security and political situations. The graduate certificate is ideal for professionals in the field or graduates who aim to bolster their understanding of the fundamentals of global justice and human security.
