Academic Handbook Current Edition LANG 0043 Skills for Arts (WSTC) Credit Points 5 Legacy Code 900110 Coordinator Eloise Tognetti Opens in new window Description This subject introduces students to the essential academic skills required for success in tertiary studies. It employs an explicit pedagogy approach to teach students how to become independent, active, and reflective learners. The subject also introduces essential research and writing skills specific to the arts and social sciences.
