Level Undergraduate Level 0 Preparatory subject Learning Outcomes On successful completion of this subject, students should be able to: Demonstrate proficiency in the above skills Subject Content 1. 2. Activities designed to improve writing ability through summary writing.
3. Activities designed to improve the ability of students to self-identify and correct grammatical and structural errors.
