- Software Development, Minor (0329)
- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- This minor covers theories and technologies used for the development of software. Students will better understand the development processes related to each phase of the software development life-cycle. In addition, they will identify, analyse, and formulate solutions to real-world problems in the software development domain.
