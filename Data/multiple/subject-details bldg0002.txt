Academic Handbook Current Edition BLDG 0002 Building Calculations (WSTC Prep) Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 700318 Coordinator Robert Paluzzano Opens in new window Description This subject is designed to assist students to become competent in the field of introductory senior mathematics. It introduces and reinforces mathematical skills in the areas of scale, application of scale, Pythagoras theory, trigonometry, sine and cosine, application of sine and cosine, building volumes, application of building volumes, gradients, application of gradients and thermal flow. Emphasis is placed on developing key competencies in building calculations.
