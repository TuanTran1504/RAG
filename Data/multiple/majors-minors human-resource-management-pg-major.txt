- Human Resource Management, Major (0134)
- Western Sydney University Major Code:
- Previous Code: SP2025.1
- Graduates of the Master of Business Administration with the Human Resource Management major are eligible for professional accreditation with the Australian Human Resources Institute (AHRI). This major equips graduates to work in the field of human resource management. Key functional areas are considered within a strategic and contextualised framework, with an underlying objective of searching for ways to strengthen organisations for all stakeholders. This education builds the necessary knowledge base for working in employment relations.
