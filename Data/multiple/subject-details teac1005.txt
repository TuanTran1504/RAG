Restrictions Students must be enrolled in 1879 Bachelor of Education (Birth – 5 Years). Learning Outcomes Engage with media and research literature regarding children’s digital worlds.
Explain children’s uses of technology from a child’s perspective, and from an adults’ perspective.
Identify the factors that influence how a child uses technology at home, in social spaces and in educational settings. 
Identify key aspects of children’s technology use that supports learning.
Describe strategies for developing curriculum using technology to support learning in early childhood. Subject Content How young children use technology
Debates surrounding young children’s technology use
Using literature to identify and analyse the factors that influence young children’s uses of technology
Safe uses of technology for young children
Technology use that supports learning in early childhood 
The role of the educator in facilitating children’s use of technology for learning
