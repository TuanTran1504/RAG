Academic Handbook Current Edition MATH 0012 Mathematics B (WSTC Prep) Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 700069 Coordinator Miriam Krakovska Opens in new window Description This subject is replaced by 700146 - Foundation Mathematics 2 (UWSCFS) from Term 1 2014. This subject has been specifically designed for students who need to refresh or upgrade their understanding of basic mathematical concepts taught in high school mathematics.  The topics include basic arithmetic and algebra, geometry, trigonometry, coordinate geometry, quadratic functions, indices, logarithms and an introduction to differential calculus.
