Academic Handbook Current Edition COMP 1014 Thinking About Data Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 301488 Coordinator Gizem Intepe Opens in new window Description This subject covers basic concepts of data centric thinking. The main areas discussed are; Populations and Samples; Sampling concepts; Types of Data; Descriptive Methods; Estimation and Inference; Modelling. The subject takes a computational and nonparametric approach, before briefly discussing theoretical concepts and distribution theory.
