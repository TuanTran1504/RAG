Academic Handbook Current Edition LANG 7010 Critical Literature Review Credit Points 20 Legacy Code 200744 Coordinator Terry Sloan Opens in new window Description The aim of this subject is to develop skills in applying rigorous analysis and critical assessment to research-based literature in general, through an examination of literature in a particular field.  This subject is intended to provide candidates with the basic skills needed to do critical analysis of literature while also enabling them to gain more familiarity with theories, issues, and problems in a particular research area.  Literature will be analysed through a balanced and constructive critique of its strengths and weaknesses, providing suggestions for how the work might have been improved.
