Academic Handbook Current Edition LAWS 2008 Government and Public Law Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 200984 Coordinator Shreeya Smith Opens in new window Description Government and Public Law introduces students to the field of public law and the operation of government in Australia. The subject has four key focus areas: Public Law in Australia, How Government Works, Government Accountability and Integrity, and Individuals and Government. The subject is intended to provide an overview of government and public law in Australia. The subject provides a foundation for the later study of Administrative Law and Constitutional Law.
