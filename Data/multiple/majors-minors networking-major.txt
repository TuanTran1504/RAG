- Western Sydney University Major Code:
- Previous Code: M3109.1
- The Networking major provides students with in-depth knowledge for the analysis, design, and implementation of networked systems. It offers students the opportunity to develop the technical skills needed for management and secure operation of a broad range of systems, including LANs, WANs, wireless and mobile networks, and large heterogeneous networks.
