Academic Handbook Current Edition BUSM 7058 Leading Contemporary Organisations Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 200833 Coordinator Marion Cornish Opens in new window Description This subject explores the processes and challenges encountered by leaders in contemporary organisations, taking into account rapidly changing environments. Through an in-depth appraisal of various leadership theories and concepts, it encourages students to reflect on the key competencies required for effective leadership. It also emphasises the importance of self-knowledge, values and ethics for contemporary leaders. Students will be required to design a leadership development program to address current and future leadership issues. Learning Outcomes On successful completion of this subject, students should be able to: Identify the key individual competencies, processes and challenges for effective leadership;
Justify the importance of values and ethics in leadership;
Apply critical, reflective and creative skills to leadership-related processes;
Forecast relevant issues and competencies in leadership for the future;
