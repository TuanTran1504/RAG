Academic Handbook Current Edition REHA 3024 Occupational Therapy Practice 3 Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 400910 Coordinator Bronwyn Gill Opens in new window Description This subject will enable students to consolidate academic knowledge and practice skills. On campus practicums will focus on occupational therapy skill development to equip students with the skills to provide occupational therapy interventions during off-site professional practice placements.  There will be opportunities to actively participate in assessment, analysis, goal setting, intervention and evaluation under the supervision of an occupational therapist. Students will experience full time work with occupational therapists in practice settings. Students will complete practice hours in accordance with World Federation of Occupational Therapy accreditation guidelines. Learning Outcomes On successful completion of this subject, students should be able to: Perform professional communication skills in the practice setting
Practise self-directed learning strategies through the use of a learning contract
Reflect on their practice experience using a structured format
Perform professional behaviours and self management in a practice setting with the guidance of an occupational therapy fieldwork supervisor
Prepare documents in a professional format related to practice under the guidance of an occupational therapy fieldwork supervisor
Conduct and interpret assessments relevant to the practice area under the guidance of an occupational therapy fieldwork supervisor
Conduct occupational therapy intervention specific to the practice area in conjunction with an occupational therapy fieldwork superviso
Utilise occupational therapy skills and techniques in practice settings Subject Content 1. Professional practice placements
