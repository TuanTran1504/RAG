Academic Handbook Current Edition GEDU 0005 Tertiary Study Skills in Construction Management (WSTC Prep) Credit Points 0 Legacy Code 700167 Coordinator Robert Paluzzano Opens in new window Description This subject is designed to assist students to become successful independent, reflective, lifelong learners. It introduces students to a range of theories and concepts to facilitate the development of practical skills and personal attitudes necessary for success in tertiary study and the workplace.
