Academic Handbook Current Edition HUMN 2011 Ancient Western Culture: Periclean Athens Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 100244 Coordinator Jennifer Mensch Opens in new window Description The Athens of Pericles is studied from three perspectives: philosophy, politics, and history. The use of reason and rhetoric is examined through the works of some pre-Socratics, Sophists and Socrates. The focus on philosophy and politics will be placed in the context of the history of the city-state and democratic citizenship.
