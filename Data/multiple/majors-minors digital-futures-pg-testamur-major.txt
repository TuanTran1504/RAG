- Western Sydney University Major Code:
- Previous code:  ST3052.1
- Digital technology is influencing practically every aspect of today’s knowledge economy and is driving advances in all sectors of society. Many jobs require broad competencies and understandings of the powerful role of technology in society. There is also an increasing demand for practical computing and software skills including for research and analysis of data. This major will allow students to develop practical as well as theoretical skills in this field.
