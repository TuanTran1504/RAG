- Creative Living for Cultural Wellbeing, Minor (0334)
- Effective from:
- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- The Creative Living for Cultural Wellbeing Minor develops students’ nuanced capabilities to engage in and curate artistic and cultural practices for a liveable and democratic global world. Students will explore interpersonal and global understandings of creativity, sustainable living practices and cultural wellbeing, with unique opportunities to become the changemakers of the future who can adapt, design, collaborate, and curate projects that contribute to cultural wellbeing in your field of expertise. Through a curated cultural event, students will bring together theoretical and experiential arts practice to develop a strengths-based approach to working with communities with notable industry and community organisations.
- This minor is a Challenge Minor, which is an innovative transdisciplinary minor co-created with students as partners. With a Challenge Minor, your learning reaches beyond your discipline, giving you the chance to amplify your learning in new directions. For more information, visit the Challenge Minor website.
