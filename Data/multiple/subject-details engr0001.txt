Academic Handbook Current Edition ENGR 0001 Academic Skills for Engineering (WSTC Prep) Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 700202 Coordinator Zdenka Misanovic Opens in new window Description This subject is designed to assist students to become successful independent reflective learners. It introduces students to a range of theories and concepts to facilitate the development of practical skills and personal attitudes necessary for success in tertiary study. Emphasis is placed on developing the key competencies of scientific inquiry - collecting, analysing, organising and communicating information as well as solving problems, particularly when related to using mathematical ideas and techniques.
