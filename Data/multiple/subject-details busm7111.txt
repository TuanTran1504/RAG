Academic Handbook Current Edition BUSM 7111 Project Management Frameworks and Methods Credit Points 10 Coordinator Samudaya Nanayakkarawasam Opens in new window Description The subject introduces frameworks, methods, approaches, and best practices employed in project management. From PMBoK, PRINCE2 to Agile, students will delve into each approach's advantages and challenges. Students will be equipped with the knowledge, skills and competencies to navigate diverse projects successfully. Students will also gain practical insights and develop decision-making skills through real-world cases and scenarios across various industries and domains.
