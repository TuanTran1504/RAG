- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- Human resources is about how leadership and management of people can support organisational objectives and create organisational opportunities. Students learn the foundations of human resource management, develop an understanding of the psychology and sociology of in a Human Resource Management context, and what that means for organisations and organisational learning and development.  Throughout the program, students are challenged to develop and demonstrate communication, cultural, and analytic skills required to be innovative and responsible team-members and leaders.
