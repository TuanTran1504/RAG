- Biomedical Engineering, Minor (0122)
- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- The Biomedical Engineering minor includes elements from the bioelectronics, biomechanical and biomechatronic specialisations. This allows students to undertake multidisciplinary study within engineering, which combines knowledge from electronics, chemical, materials, mechanical and mechatronic engineering with the life sciences of medicine, biology and molecular biology. Graduates will be equipped with professional skills to work in biomedical industry as engineers, with a good understanding of multidisciplinary principles and practices.
- Location
- Minor Structure
- This minor is only available to students enrolled in Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) (3740) and Bachelor of Engineering Advanced (Honours) (3771).
