Academic Handbook Current Edition MATH 2011 Making Sense of Data Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 301032 Coordinator Neil Hopkins Opens in new window Description The subject builds on the basic statistical concepts introduced in first year, and also prepares students for broader application of statistics for those majoring in science or business. Topics include hypothesis testing; analysis of categorical data; analysis of variance; non-parametric methods; re-sampling (cross validation/bootstrapping); Introduction to visual data analysis; simple Multivariate statistics and sampling and design.
