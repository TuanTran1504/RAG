Academic Handbook Current Edition LAWS 1007 Introduction to Law (WSTC) Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 700157 Coordinator Bader Unnisa Mehdi Opens in new window Description This subject introduces students to the Australian legal system. It considers the historical and legal factors which influenced its development and the key institutions and processes which have resulted. It considers the impact of Australian law and legal system on Indigenous Australians and their laws. The subject introduces students to legal method: legal reasoning and statutory interpretation. Students will also develop academic literacies, including study management, writing and referencing in a legal context.
