Academic Handbook Current Edition NATS 0004 Academic Skills for Science (WSTC) Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 900101 Coordinator Phillip Newman Opens in new window Description This subject is designed to assist students to become successful independent reflective learners.  It introduces students to a range of theories and concepts to facilitate the development of practical skills and personal attitudes necessary for success in tertiary study.  Emphasis is placed on developing the key competencies of scientific enquiry - collecting, analysing, organising and communicating information as well as solving problems, particularly when related to using mathematical ideas and techniques. Locate and use information technology systems and educational technologies to support learning and assessment
