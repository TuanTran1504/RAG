Academic Handbook Current Edition MKTG 0001 Marketing Skills Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 201035 Coordinator Sabina Yasmin Opens in new window Description This subject delivers an overview of the marketing process and how it works within the business environment. Marketing segmentation and market positioning, new product development branding, channels, promotion and advertising plus the concept of marketing as a social system are areas of study. The subject provides a broad overview and a general understanding of marketing concepts and marketing within a social context. School Business Discipline Marketing Student Contribution Band HECS Band 4 10cp Check your fees via the Fees page. Level Undergraduate Level 0 Preparatory subject Restrictions Students must be enrolled in 2845 Masters Qualifying Program with Business English. Learning Outcomes On successful completion of this subject, students should be able to: Identify important marketing concepts and frameworks;
Apply marketing concepts and frameworks to case based situations;
Demonstrate how marketing creates value in a societal context; and
Demonstrate English language skills in the key IELTS test areas: writing, speaking, listening and reading.
