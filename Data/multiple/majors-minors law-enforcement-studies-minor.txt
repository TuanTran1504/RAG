- Western Sydney University Minor Code:
- The Law Enforcement Studies minor offers students an opportunity to distinguish themselves through a specialisation in Policing. The minor equips students with advanced knowledge of modern policing practice and theory. Graduates will develop analytical and problem-solving skills, as well as client-focused approaches to serving diverse communities. The knowledge and skills offered by the minor enhance employability in the law enforcement sector.
