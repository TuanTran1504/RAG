Academic Handbook Current Edition ELEC 3010 Renewable Energy Systems Design Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 301425 Coordinator Ali Hellany Opens in new window Description This subject prepares engineering students to be conversant with renewable energy systems. Students will learn to appraise environmental, social, legal, economic and political issues concerning renewable energy systems. Students will also learn relevant design skills related to renewable energy systems. ENGR 1011 Equivalent Subjects ELEC 4006 Sustainable Energy Systems Learning Outcomes On successful completion of this subject, students should be able to: Analyse the technical aspects of renewable energy systems.
Design renewable energy systems for a given set of conditions.
Assess the social, legal, policy, political economic and environmental issues associated with renewable energy systems.
Demonstrate communication and collaboration skills in working with others in an ethical and respectful manner to produce professional analyses and reports Subject Content Introduction and principles of renewable energy
