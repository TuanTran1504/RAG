Academic Handbook Current Edition MKTG 3017 The Markets of Asia Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 200098 Coordinator Sabreena Amin Opens in new window Description Markets of Asia focuses on internationalisation and global competitiveness of organisations in the Asian region. The subject also encourages an appreciation of cultural diversity, and develops students' knowledge and skills so that upon completion of this subject, they will understand the relevant business practices needed to be responsive to enterprise opportunities and threats within this global community. Learning Outcomes On successful completion of this subject, students should be able to: Demonstrate an understanding of the growing importance and strategic emergence of the markets of Asia within a global context;
Evaluate the cultural, political and institutional norms and their dynamic impacts on international businesses strategies;
Apply analytical, interpersonal and decision-making skills appropriate to formulate successful international business strategies. Subject Content - introduction to The markets of Asia
