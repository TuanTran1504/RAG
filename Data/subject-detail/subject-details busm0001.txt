Academic Handbook Current Edition BUSM 0001 Academic Skills for Business (WSTC Prep) Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 700248 Coordinator Abdullah Al Aabed Opens in new window Description This subject introduces students to the essential academic skills required for success in tertiary studies. It employs an explicit pedagogical approach to teach students how to become independent, active, and reflective learners. The subject also includes essential research and writing skills specific to the study of Business. School Western Sydney The College Discipline Business and Management, Not Elsewhere Classified.
