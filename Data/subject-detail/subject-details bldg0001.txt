Academic Handbook Current Edition BLDG 0001 Academic Skills for Construction Management (WSTC Prep) Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 700200 Coordinator Robert Paluzzano Opens in new window Description This subject is designed to assist students to become successful independent and reflective learners. It introduces students to a range of theories and concepts to facilitate the development of practical skills and personal attitudes necessary for success in tertiary study. The subject also develops basic competencies in using computers and application software for the Building Design Management and Construction Management industries.  The subject covers the use of word-processing software and spreadsheets. Students are also introduced to project management and design software. Locate and use information technology systems and educational technologies to support learning and assessment.
