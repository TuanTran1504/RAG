Academic Handbook Current Edition REHA 2010 Adult Speech and Language Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 401302 Coordinator Leigha Dark Opens in new window Description This subject discusses speech and language development and change from young adults to the elderly. It focuses on acquired communication disorders of neurological origin, such as aphasia and related disorders, apraxia of speech and dysarthria, and the diagnosis and treatment of serious communication disorders.
