Academic Handbook Current Edition LANG 0002 Academic Communication 2 (WSTC Prep) Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 700199 Coordinator Richard Robinson Opens in new window Description This subject is designed to expand upon and extend the academic literacy skills acquired in Academic Communication 1. The subject will assist students to develop the skills required to critically read and analyse a variety of texts, and to develop their research and writing skills to produce complex texts. There is a particular focus in this subject on critique and analysis in the process of understanding and producing academic texts.
