Academic Handbook Current Edition HOSP 3001 Approved Industrial Experience (400 Hrs) Credit Points 0 Legacy Code HS305A Coordinator Tim Hall Opens in new window Description This is a "Work Experience in Industry" subject, for which no student contribution or fee is charged. Enrolment in the subject will not consume Student Learning Entitlement (SLE). Students in the Bachelor of Hospitality Management are required to obtain at least 400 hours, vocationally relevant, industrial experience during their program of study.
