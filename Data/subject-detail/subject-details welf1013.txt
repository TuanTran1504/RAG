Academic Handbook Current Edition WELF 1013 Counselling Foundations (Block) Credit Points 10 Coordinator Jack Isherwood Opens in new window Description This subject introduces students to key concepts and theories of counselling and other therapeutic approaches. Students will also develop an understanding of counselling practice in an Australian context and will learn how to apply their knowledge of subject content to their own life experiences and real world examples. Students will also develop the capacity to critically evaluate the strengths and weaknesses of different counselling approaches.
