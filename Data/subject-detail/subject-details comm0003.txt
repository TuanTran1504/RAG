Academic Handbook Current Edition COMM 0003 Academic Skills for Design (WSTC Prep) Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 700221 Coordinator Ben Fletcher Opens in new window Description This subject introduces students to the essential academic skills required for success in tertiary studies. It employs an explicit pedagogy approach to teach students how to become independent, active, and reflective learners. The subject also includes essential research and writing skills specific to the Diploma in Design. Locate and use information technology systems and educational technologies to support learning and assessment.
