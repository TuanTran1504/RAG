Academic Handbook Current Edition LAWS 3018 Equity & Trusts Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 200757 Coordinator Maria Bhatti Opens in new window Description This subject examines the principles of the law of equity and trusts in Australia. The primary emphasis is on the nature and development of the law of equity and students are also introduced to fundamental principles in the law of trusts in a culturally diverse society. Topics include the nature and application of principles and maxims of Equity, especially in relation to First Nations People, the trust, the types of trusts and introduction to express trusts. Examine complex policy and practical debates surrounding equity and trusts;
Apply the principles of equity and trusts to analysing and solving hypothetical problems and theoretical legal issues particularly in relation to First Nations People
