Academic Handbook Current Edition BIOS 1010 Bioscience 1 Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 401002 Coordinator Anna Maceri Opens in new window Description This subject will be replaced from Autumn 2022 by 401469 Bioscience 1. This subject introduces nursing and midwifery students to the terminology and major introductory concepts related to normal structure and function of the human body and its relationship to performances of activities of living and healthy lifestyle practices.
