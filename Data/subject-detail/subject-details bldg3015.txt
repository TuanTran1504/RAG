Academic Handbook Current Edition BLDG 3015 Contract Management Credit Points 10 Coordinator Wenchi Shou Opens in new window Description This subject provides students with the knowledge and skills to manage building contracts, expanding on knowledge gained in the first year core subject Contract Administration. Students learn the professional practice skills for quantity surveying and contract administration services during construction. Knowledge gained in this subject will benefit students aspiring to careers such as Contract Managers and Quantity Surveyors.
