Academic Handbook Current Edition ECON 3021 The Superannuation Industry Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 200077 Coordinator John Ablett Opens in new window Description This subject provides students with an understanding of the economics of retirement and retirement income provision, with particular emphasis on the Australian superannuation industry. On completion of this subject, students should have a comprehensive understanding of superannuation in Australia, as well as an appreciation of the economic issues associated with alternative models of retirement income provision.
