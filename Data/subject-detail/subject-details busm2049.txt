Academic Handbook Current Edition BUSM 2049 Creative and Innovative Thinkers Credit Points 10 Coordinator Carla Duarte de Jesus Dias Wadewitz Opens in new window Description This subject explores how to use creativity and innovation skills to solve real world problems and provides students with opportunities to work on projects directly with industry. Through practical, hands-on activities, students work through ideas using well-recognised and used tools and techniques. 
Innovation and creativity skills are highly sought after globally across all sectors. Creativity is increasingly important as a fundamental differentiating skill for graduates and ranks in the top three employability skills. This subject is recommended for all students in all programs who want to establish continuous collaborative engagement with industry and grow personal, career, self-employment and networking opportunities.
