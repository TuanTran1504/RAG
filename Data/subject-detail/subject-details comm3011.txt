Academic Handbook Current Edition COMM 3011 Creative TEAMS 1 Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 102431 Coordinator Kate Richards Opens in new window Description Creative TEAMS 1 will bring students together in interdisciplinary groups to work with Creative Industries practitioners in a 'real world' context. Students will learn how to collaborate to develop an original solution to a "wicked" problem set by the industry partner. Through research and planning, in relation to a set of relevant constraints: such as cultural, social, environmental, site and audience, students will develop a strategy that utilises the appropriate media, message and approach, to address the identified target audience, based on research findings. School Humanities & Comm Arts Discipline Communication and Media Studies, Not Elsewhere Classified. Student Contribution Band HECS Band 4 10cp Check your fees via the Fees page. Level Undergraduate Level 3 subject Restrictions Students must be enrolled in Bachelor of Creative Industries or associated double degrees.Successful completion of 80 credit points at Level 1 in currently enrolled program. Learning Outcomes On successful completion of this subject, students should be able to: Identify the elements of an ill-structured problem
Undertake appropriate research in collaboration with others
Use design thinking to reflect on a real world problem
Use critical thinking to reflect on research
Work in teams to develop novel solutions
Present findings in a range of formats
Demonstrate professional communication skills Subject Content Design thinking
Research skills
Critical thinking
Collaborative problem solving
Professional communication strategies
