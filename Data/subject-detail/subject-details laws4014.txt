Academic Handbook Current Edition LAWS 4014 Remedies Credit Points 10 Coordinator Adrian Coorey Opens in new window Description This subject examines the principles of the major common law, equitable, restitutionary and statutory remedies available in Australia to wronged parties in a variety of circumstances. Emphasis is on the analysis of practical and theoretical advantages and disadvantages of various remedies in a culturally diverse society. Topics include the effectiveness of common law, equitable and statutory remedies and the law of restitution and the scope of each of these remedies.
