Academic Handbook Current Edition MKTG 7011 Feasibility Modelling Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 201014 Coordinator Za Manaf Opens in new window Description This subject emphasises the evaluation of a site for the purposes of selecting development projects, evaluating different options and making the decision as to whether to proceed to a more detailed feasibility study and modelling. The subject provides students with the knowledge and skills to prepare a full feasibility report and analysis of a project's concept and financial modelling. Students will apply theories and concepts to a case study using current and available applications.
