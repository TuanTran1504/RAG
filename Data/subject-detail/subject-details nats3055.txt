Academic Handbook Current Edition NATS 3055 Practicum 1 Credit Points 10 Coordinator Nijat Imin Opens in new window Description Practicum 1 will develop your job-readiness in areas of practical skills, problem solving, critical thinking, and teamwork.  Working in real-world situations you will apply your knowledge of theory, experimental design, methodology, and analysis, in your discipline area to solving defined problems. The subject will provide you with the opportunity to initially discuss and plan methods to solve a defined problem, and then using laboratory experiments, fieldwork, or other activities relevant to professional practice, to test your approach to the problem.
