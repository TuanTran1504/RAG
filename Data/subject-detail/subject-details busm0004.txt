Academic Handbook Current Edition BUSM 0004 Business Ethics (WSTC Prep) Credit Points 5 Legacy Code 700215 Coordinator Vlad Krajsic Opens in new window Description Business Ethics seeks to give students an understanding of the concept of ethics in general, the role ethical thinking and behaviour plays in the contemporary business environment and the importance of critical and analytical thinking.  Through an examination of the historical development of ethics and the most recognised ethical approaches, students will develop skills in responding to ethical questions and the use of applied ethics.  Students will also be given the opportunity to develop skills in relation to selecting and evaluating sources and materials when preparing tasks and critical and analytical thinking.
