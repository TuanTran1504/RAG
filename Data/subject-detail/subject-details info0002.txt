Academic Handbook Current Edition INFO 0002 Academic Skills for Information Communications Technology (WSTC) Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 900100 Coordinator Buddhima De Silva Opens in new window Description This subject is designed to assist students to become successful independent reflective learners. It introduces students to a range of theories and concepts to facilitate the development of practical skills and personal attitudes necessary for success in tertiary study. Emphasis is placed on developing the key competencies required to be successful in an Information Communications Tschnology course- research, analysis, problem solving, communication and team work.
