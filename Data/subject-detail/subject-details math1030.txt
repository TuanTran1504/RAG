Academic Handbook Current Edition MATH 1030 Statistics for Business Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 200032 Coordinator Neil Hopkins Opens in new window Description Statistics for Business introduces the basic concepts and techniques of statistics that are particularly relevant to problem solving in business. It also provides a sound base for more advanced study in statistics and forecasting in subsequent sessions. Topics include: presentation of data; descriptive statistics; the role of uncertainty in business decision making; hypothesis testing; and basic forecasting.
