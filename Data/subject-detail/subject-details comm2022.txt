Academic Handbook Current Edition COMM 2022 Journalism: Research and Investigation Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 102017 Coordinator Ming Diao Opens in new window Description Investigation and research are fundamental to journalism and many other forms of writing. This subject provides students with the skills to dig deeper to uncover information with which they can build stories that have depth and impact. Students will analyse the role and power of investigative journalism within the information media industry.
