Academic Handbook Current Edition BUSM 3061 Project Management Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 300727 Coordinator Wenchi Shou Opens in new window Description This subject is to give students an understanding of appropriate methods of managing construction projects and to develop skills in using these methods on the type of projects the students expect to undertake in their professional careers. Learning Outcomes Apply organisational behaviour theory to specific construction management projects
Manage a construction project to meet cost, time, scope and quality constraints
Assess and manage risk in a construction project
Establish and manage communication and procurement protocols in a construction project
