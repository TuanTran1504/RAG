Academic Handbook Current Edition REHA 4026 Professional Issues Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 401312 Coordinator Leigha Dark Opens in new window Description Covering a range of ethical and professional issues for speech pathologists, the Professional Issues subject includes recent professional developments in speech pathology relevant to entry level practitioners, ethical and medico-legal issues, registration as a speech pathologist, communication and working with others (including interprofessional issues), safety, continuing education, workload control strategies, duty of care responsibilities, quality improvement processes (including critical reflection), employment strategies and career pathways.
