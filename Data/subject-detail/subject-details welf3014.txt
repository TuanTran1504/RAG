Academic Handbook Current Edition WELF 3014 Social Work and Social Policy Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 400511 Coordinator Robyn North Opens in new window Description This subject examines the context and challenges for social policy in Australia and the relationship between social policy and social work practice. Topics covered include: fundamental debates in welfare policy; comparative and historical contexts in how welfare policy is developed; the impacts of remedial welfare policy and policy solutions to disadvantage; and strategies to influence policy development related to social work practice.
