Academic Handbook Current Edition MGMT 7003 Financial Management of Projects Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 301194 Coordinator Xiaohua Jin Opens in new window Description Projects are always unique and their processes are always subject to elements of uncertainty and change. The skill of project management professionals in securing the best return for their organisation's efforts is thus held in great value. This subject is concerned with financial aspects of project management from both theoretical and practical perspectives. The aim is to explain how financial management tasks are carried out in the context of projects as well as stimulate students' reflection on best practice in project financial management.
