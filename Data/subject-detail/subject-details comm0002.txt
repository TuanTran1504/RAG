Academic Handbook Current Edition COMM 0002 Academic Skills for Communication (WSTC Prep) Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 700218 Coordinator Chantal Rozairo Opens in new window Description This subject introduces students to the essential academic skills required for success in tertiary studies. It employs an explicit pedagogy approach to teach students how to become independent, active, and reflective learners. The subject also includes essential research and writing skills specific to the Communication Arts. School Western Sydney The College Discipline Communication and Media Studies, Not Elsewhere Classified. Student Contribution Band HECS Band 4 10cp Check your fees via the Fees page. Level Undergraduate Level 0 Preparatory subject Restrictions Learning Outcomes On successful completion of this subject, students should be able to: Use both independent and collaborative learning strategies to advance the knowledge of the individual and that of their peers.
Communicate effectively through the development of critical reading, listening, speaking and writing skills in context.
Locate and use information technology systems and educational technologies to support learning and assessment.
Apply critical thinking, and use problem solving skills and research skills, to make informed decisions.
