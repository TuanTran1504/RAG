Academic Handbook Current Edition CIVL 4016 Envelope and Services Credit Points 10 Legacy Code 301222 Coordinator Keivan Bamdad Masouleh Opens in new window Description The building envelope and its inter-relationship with building services are critical to the successful functioning of our modern built environment. In this subject students will learn how to minimise operational energy requirements by making good choices in terms of materials and systems which focus on end-user needs. Measurement of building performance and continuous improvement is addressed alongside compliance with building codes and relevant Australian standards.
